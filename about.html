About AdMediaPixeel LLC

A digital agency built on quality, compliance, and results.

Who We Are

AdMediaPixeel LLC is a United States-based digital agency registered in the state of Wyoming. We operate at the intersection of mobile technology and digital marketing, helping businesses and consumers through thoughtfully designed applications and strategic growth initiatives.

Founded with the mission to deliver reliable, high-quality digital products, our company brings together professionals with backgrounds in software engineering, product design, and performance marketing. We take a measured, detail-oriented approach to every project we undertake.

What We Do

Our core business focuses on three areas:

Mobile Application Development. We design, develop, and maintain mobile applications for iOS. Each application we build follows Apple's Human Interface Guidelines and is developed with user privacy, performance, and accessibility in mind. We work across categories including productivity, utilities, lifestyle, and entertainment.

App Publishing & Management. We handle the full app publishing lifecycle — from initial App Store submission and review to ongoing version management, compliance updates, and metadata optimization. We ensure that every application we publish meets or exceeds the standards required by Apple's App Store Review Guidelines.

Performance Marketing. We plan and execute digital marketing campaigns focused on measurable outcomes. Our team works with paid acquisition channels, analytics platforms, and attribution tools to deliver transparent reporting and sustainable growth for the applications and brands we support.

Our Approach

We believe in building things properly. That means writing clean code, designing intuitive interfaces, respecting user data, and maintaining full compliance with the platforms we operate on. We prioritize long-term quality over short-term metrics.

Transparency is central to how we operate. Our privacy policies, terms of service, and support channels are maintained to provide users with clear information about how our products work and how their data is handled.

Our Principles

Quality First

Every application and campaign we produce is held to rigorous internal quality standards before it reaches users.

Compliance

We design our products and processes around platform guidelines, privacy regulations, and industry best practices.

Transparency

We communicate clearly with our users, partners, and platforms through comprehensive policies and responsive support.

User Privacy

We treat user data with the care and respect it deserves. Data minimization and clear consent are non-negotiable.

Reliability

We build products that work consistently and maintain our services with regular updates and proactive monitoring.

Accountability

We stand behind our products. Our support team is accessible, and we address issues promptly and thoroughly.
