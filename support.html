Subscription & Billing

All purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. To manage your subscriptions, cancel a renewal, or request a refund:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions
  3. Select the relevant subscription to manage or cancel it

For refund requests, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can reach our support team by sending an email to support@admediapixel.com. Please include the name of the app, your device model, iOS version, and a description of the issue. We will respond within 24–48 business hours.

Subscriptions purchased through the App Store are managed by Apple. To cancel, go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iOS device. Select the subscription you wish to cancel and follow the on-screen instructions. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the purchase you would like to be refunded for. Apple will review and process your request.

Our data collection practices vary by application. In general, we may collect device information, usage analytics, and crash reports to improve our products. We do not sell personal information. For complete details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

If you would like to request the deletion of your account or personal data associated with one of our applications, please send an email to support@admediapixel.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." Include the app name and any account details. We will process your request in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
