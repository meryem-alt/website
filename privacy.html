Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: April 16, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AdMediaPixeel LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile applications, websites, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

AdMediaPixeel LLC is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in a transparent and responsible manner. This policy applies to all users of our Services regardless of location.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect information that you provide to us voluntarily, including but not limited to:

  • Contact information such as your name and email address when you submit a support request or contact form.
  • Account information if you create an account within any of our mobile applications.
  • Feedback, reviews, or correspondence you send to us.
  • Payment information processed through third-party payment providers (such as Apple's in-app purchase system). We do not directly store credit card or payment details.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device information: device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and device settings.
  • Usage data: app usage patterns, features accessed, session duration, and interaction data.
  • Log data: IP addresses, browser type, referring URLs, pages viewed, and access timestamps.
  • Crash reports and performance data to help us identify and resolve technical issues.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

We may receive information about you from third-party services that you connect to our applications, analytics providers, or advertising platforms. This information is used in accordance with the terms described in this policy.

1.4 Photos, Face Data, and Visual Content

Our app includes a suite of AI-powered creative features. When you use any of these features, you may choose to upload a photo from your device. That photo may contain your likeness or the likeness of other individuals ("face data"). Uploading a photo is always voluntary and only occurs when you actively initiate a request. The features that may involve photo uploads include:

  • AI Video Generation: Animate a photo into a video using AI.
  • Motion Control Video: Generate a video from a photo with customizable motion direction and style.
  • Cartoon & Anime Style: Transform a photo into a cartoon or anime-styled image.
  • Art Style Filters: Apply creative styles such as oil painting, watercolor, sketch, and others to a photo.
  • Image-to-Image Transformation: Use a photo as a reference to generate a new AI image.
  • Other Image Generation Features: Any additional AI image generation features that accept a photo as input.

Collection: We collect only the photos you voluntarily submit when using the above features. We do not perform facial recognition, extract biometric identifiers, build facial profiles, or analyze the identity of individuals in uploaded photos. Face data is never used for advertising, profiling, identity verification, or any purpose other than completing the specific creative request you initiated.

Use: Uploaded photos are used solely to process the creative request you have chosen — such as generating a video, applying an art style, or producing an AI image. Photos are never used to train AI models, build user profiles, or serve advertisements.

Third-party sharing and storage: To fulfill your request, your uploaded photo is transmitted to one or more of the following third-party AI processing providers, depending on the feature used:

  • PiAPI / Kling AI (Kuaishou Technology): Used for AI video generation and motion control features. Input images and output files are retained for up to 3 days on their servers and automatically deleted thereafter. Kling AI stores data on servers located in Singapore and does not use API-submitted content to train its AI models.
  • LightX Editor API (Andor Communications Private Limited): Used for AI image generation features including cartoon, anime, art style filters, and image-to-image transformation. Uploaded images are automatically deleted within 24 hours of processing and are not used for model training. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

We do not sell, rent, or share your photos or face data with any party other than the processors listed above, and only for the purpose of fulfilling your specific request.

Retention: We do not store your uploaded photos on our own servers beyond what is required to submit your request. Third-party processors delete your content automatically — PiAPI/Kling AI within 3 days, LightX Editor API within 24 hours. No face data is retained by us or our processors beyond these periods.

No biometric data collection: We do not extract, store, or process biometric identifiers or biometric information (such as facial geometry or facial templates) from uploaded photos, regardless of which feature is used. No biometric data derived from face data is created or retained at any stage of processing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our mobile applications and related services.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide technical assistance.
  • Analytics and Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical problems, and improve the functionality and user experience of our Services.
  • Communication: To send you important notifications related to our Services, including updates, security alerts, and changes to our policies.
  • Compliance: To comply with applicable legal obligations, enforce our terms, and protect the rights and safety of our users and company.
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and Services may use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with our Services. These technologies help us remember your preferences, analyze website traffic, measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns, and provide a more personalized user experience.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features of our Services.

3.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic operation of our website and Services. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous usage data.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced functionality and personalization based on your interactions with our Services.

4. Third-Party Services

We may integrate or use third-party services in connection with our Services. Third-party services we may use include:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution, in-app purchases, and subscription management.
  • PiAPI / Kling AI: For AI video generation and motion control processing. See Section 1.4 for full details.
  • LightX Editor API: For AI image generation and style processing. See Section 1.4 for full details.
  • Analytics Providers: Such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar services.
  • Crash Reporting Tools: Such as Firebase Crashlytics or similar services.
  • Advertising Networks: We may use advertising SDKs or services to deliver relevant advertisements within our applications.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: We use secure cloud hosting services to store and process data.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies before providing any personal information.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our Services, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of AdMediaPixeel LLC, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets.
  • With Your Consent: When you have given us explicit consent to share your information for a specific purpose.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by applicable law. Specific retention periods include:

  • Photos and face data (AI features): Not stored on our servers. Deleted by third-party processors within 24–72 hours. See Section 1.4.
  • Support communications: Retained for up to 2 years.
  • Analytics data: Retained in aggregated or anonymized form for up to 24 months.

When we no longer need your personal information, we will securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention procedures.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect the information we collect. These measures include encryption of data in transit, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you become aware of any security incident involving your personal information, please contact us immediately at support@admediapixel.com.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, object to certain processing, withdraw consent, or opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@admediapixel.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@admediapixel.com.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the country in which you reside, including Singapore (where Kling AI servers are located) and the United States. We take appropriate measures to ensure that your personal information remains protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy when transferred internationally.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information), and non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights.

To submit a CCPA request, please contact us at support@admediapixel.com.

12. European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The legal bases for our processing include consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, and legal obligation.

You may contact us to exercise your rights under the GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
